Main Course

Burger

This is a classic handmade beef burger, with white sesame seeds sprinkled on the bun and a caramelized crust after being baked. The ingredients are fresh avocado slices, some onions, and crispy fried beef, and finally a spoonful of mayonnaise.

Pizza

A round pizza boasts a perfectly baked crust topped with a vibrant tomato sauce base and generously sprinkled with melted mozzarella cheese. It is also topped with ham slices, roasted garlic and pepperoni.

Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak consists of steamed rice with coconut milk, fragrant pandan leaves, chili sauce, fried peanuts and anchovies, cooling cucumber slices and half a boiled egg. The traditional condiments are hot and spicy in nature and are wrapped in a double layer of banana leaves in a pyramid shape.

Beverage

Honey Vanilla Milkshake

Rich and creamy milkshake flavored with honey and vanilla.

Cola

A classic, refreshing cola drink-crisp and carbonated with a hint of sweetness.

Coffee

A concentrated form of caffe latte produced by forcing hot water. It is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Dessert

Ice-cream

Three different flavors of ice cream such as vanilla flavoured, strawberry flavoured, and chocolate flavoured.

Cake

Chocolate cream cake is decorated with a circle of chocolate cream flowers, showing an elegant spiral texture. The top edge of the cake is dotted with black chocolate crumbs, adding a sense of layering and taste. The sides of the cake are covered with light brown cream.

Honey Drizzle Donuts

Soft, fluffy baked honey donuts with a flavorful vanilla bean-honey glaze. It's perfect dessert for those who just finish the main course.
